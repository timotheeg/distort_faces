Instructions

  1. Only tested and expected to work on google chrome desktop - NOT mobile!!
  2. Roll over with mouse on image to play with a pic
  3. Click the canvas to freeze the blob, click again to have the blob follow your mouse again
  4. Click the links above to save your creation
  5. Play with the settings above (best if you have frozen rendering first!)
  6. Keyboard controls:
    • Press escape to compare with original
    • Press space to freeze the current blob and control a new blob!
    • Press backspace to remove previously frozen blob
    • Press enter to clear all blobs
    • Note: multi blob only work if they do not overlap!
  7. Pics are put on the canvas center-cropped, do preprocessing/cropping on your images before using this.
